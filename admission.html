Admission Information

Admission Procedure

The admission process is conducted after the evaluation of the physical documents and as per the age of the student according to the National Academic policy 2023.

Offline Procedure:

  1. Visit the school office between 9:00 AM to 12:45 PM on working days
  2. Collect the admission form and prospectus
  3. Submit the completed form along with required documents
  4. Pay the admission fees to confirm the seat

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate (for new admissions)
  • Transfer Certificate (for students coming from other schools)
  • Aadhar Card of student and parents
  • Previous year's report card
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options to suit parents' convenience: Students can pay fees in four instalments.

Online Payment:

  • Through our secure payment gateway on the school website
  • UPI payment to school's UPI ID: blessing@upi
  • Net Banking/NEFT/RTGS

Offline Payment:

  • Cash payment at school office
  • Cheque/Demand Draft in favor of "Blessing English Medium School"

Fee structure varies according to grade level. Please contact the office for detailed fee information.

Scholarships

The school facilitates various scholarships provided by the Gujarat government:

1. RTE full scholarship for government approved categories.

The Right to Education (RTE) Act provides free and compulsory education to children between 6 and 14 years old. Under this act, 25% of seats in private schools are reserved for financially unstable students. To avail of RTE admission, applicants must be permanent residents of India and meet specific eligibility criteria

2. Namo Laxmi, Namaste Saraswati scholarships for girl child from std 9 to 12.

3. Gyansadhana scheme

Scheme for Secondary students who pass the required examination. This is for meritorious students of classes 11-12 with a family income of less than 6 lakhs per annum.

Apart from the government scholarships, the school provides scholarships to all categories of students who are in need and have good academic performance.
